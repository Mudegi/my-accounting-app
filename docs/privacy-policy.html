YourBooks — Privacy Policy

Last updated: March 4, 2026

YourBooks ("we," "our," or "us") is committed to protecting the privacy of our users. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, store, and protect your information when you use the YourBooks mobile application.

1. Information We Collect

1.1 Account Information

When you create an account, we collect:

  • Full name
  • Email address
  • Password (stored securely using industry-standard hashing)
  • Phone number (optional)

1.2 Business Information

To set up your business profile, we collect:

  • Business name
  • Tax Identification Number (TIN) — optional
  • Business address and contact details
  • Branch information (for multi-branch businesses)

1.3 Transaction Data

When you use the app for your business operations, we process:

  • Sales and purchase records
  • Product and inventory information
  • Customer and supplier names and contact details
  • Payment method types (e.g., cash, mobile money)
  • Expense records
  • Accounting journal entries

1.4 Device Information

We may automatically collect:

  • Device type and operating system version
  • App version
  • Crash logs and performance data (via Expo)

1.5 Camera Access

YourBooks requests camera permission solely for barcode scanning functionality. We do not capture, store, or transmit photos or videos.

2. How We Use Your Information

We use your information exclusively to:

  • Provide and maintain the YourBooks application services
  • Process and display your business transactions, sales, and accounting data
  • Generate business reports, receipts, and financial summaries
  • Manage your subscription and account
  • Provide customer support
  • Improve our application and fix bugs

We do NOT:

  • Sell your personal or business data to third parties
  • Use your data for advertising or marketing purposes
  • Share your financial records with anyone without your consent
  • Track your location

3. Data Storage and Security

Your data is stored securely using Supabase, a trusted cloud database platform built on PostgreSQL. We implement the following security measures:

  • Row-Level Security (RLS): Each business can only access its own data — no business can see another's records
  • Encrypted connections: All data transmitted between the app and our servers uses HTTPS/TLS encryption
  • Secure authentication: Passwords are hashed using bcrypt; we never store plain-text passwords
  • Role-based access: Users within a business have defined roles (admin, manager, cashier) with appropriate permissions

4. Data Sharing

We do not share your data with third parties, except:

  • Service providers: We use Supabase for data hosting and Expo/EAS for app delivery. These services process data only as necessary to provide their services to us.
  • Legal requirements: We may disclose data if required by law, court order, or government regulation.
  • EFRIS/URA integration: If you choose to enable EFRIS (Electronic Fiscal Receipting and Invoicing System) integration, relevant invoice data is transmitted to the Uganda Revenue Authority as required by Ugandan tax law. This is initiated only by your action.

5. Data Retention

Your business data is retained for as long as your account is active. If you delete your account:

  • Your personal account information will be deleted within 30 days
  • Business transaction data may be retained for up to 7 years as required by Ugandan tax and accounting regulations
  • You may request full data deletion by contacting us

6. Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Access your personal and business data stored in the app
  • Correct inaccurate information through the app's settings
  • Export your data (sales reports, transaction history) from within the app
  • Delete your account and request removal of your data
  • Withdraw consent at any time by stopping use of the app

7. Children's Privacy

YourBooks is a business application designed for users aged 18 and above. We do not knowingly collect data from children under 18. If we become aware that a child under 18 has provided personal data, we will delete it promptly.

8. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any significant changes by displaying a notice in the app. The "Last updated" date at the top of this page reflects the most recent revision.

9. Contact Us

If you have any questions or concerns about this Privacy Policy or your data, please contact us:

  • Email: support@yourbooksapp.com
  • App: Use the Help & Support section within YourBooks
