YourBooks — Delete Your Account

Request account and data deletion

If you would like to delete your YourBooks account and all associated data, you can do so by following the steps below.

How to Delete Your Account

Option 1: From within the app

  • Open YourBooks
  • Go to Settings (bottom tab)
  • Scroll down and tap "Delete Account"
  • Confirm the deletion when prompted

Option 2: Email request

  • Send an email to support@yourbooksapp.com
  • Subject: Account Deletion Request
  • Include the email address associated with your YourBooks account
  • We will process your request within 7 business days

What Gets Deleted

When you delete your account, the following data will be permanently removed:

  • Your personal profile information (name, email, phone)
  • Your business profile and settings
  • All sales and transaction records
  • Product and inventory data
  • Customer and supplier records
  • Accounting entries and reports
  • Subscription and payment history

Data Retention

In compliance with Ugandan tax and accounting regulations, certain financial records may be retained for up to 7 years after account deletion. These records are anonymized and cannot be linked back to your personal identity.

Contact Us

If you have any questions about account deletion, please contact us at support@yourbooksapp.com.
